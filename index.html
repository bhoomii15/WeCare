What are some common issues us women face?
Relax, we got you!

  1. 01

    PCOS/PCOD - Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a condition in which the ovaries produce an abnormal amount of androgens, male sex hormones that are usually present in women in small amounts. Common symptoms: Missed / Irregular periods,excess body hair, weight gain, male-pattern baldness or thinning of hair.

  2. 02

    Anemia - Anemia is a condition that develops when your blood produces a lower-than-normal amount of healthy red blood cells. If you have anemia, your body does not get enough oxygen-rich blood. The lack of oxygen can make you feel tired or weak. Common symptoms: Extreme fatigue, weakness. pale skin, chest pain, fast heartbeat or shortness of breath, headache, dizziness.

  3. 03

    Breast Cancer - Breast cancer is cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts. Common symptoms: A breast lump or thickening that feels different from the surrounding tissue, change in the size/shape/appearance of a breast,inverted nipple, peeling/scaling/crusting/flaking of the areola or breast skin, redness or pitting of the skin over your breast, like the skin of an orange. It's wise to see a doctor when you find a lump or other change in your breast.

  4. 04

    Urinary tract infection - A urinary tract infection (UTI) is an infection in any part of the urinary system. Most infections involve the lower urinary tract — the bladder and the urethra. Common Symptoms : burning feeling when urinating, strong-smelling urine, pelvic pain.
