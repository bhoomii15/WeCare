What are some common issues us women face?
Relax, we got you!

  1. 01

    PCOS, or polycystic ovary syndrome, involves ovaries overproducing androgens, causing irregular periods, excessive body hair, weight gain, male-pattern baldness, and hair thinning in women.

  2. 02

    Anemia results from reduced healthy red blood cell production, leading to oxygen deprivation, causing fatigue, weakness, pale skin, chest pain, rapid heartbeat, headache, and dizziness.

  3. 03

    Breast cancer originates in breast cells and may manifest as a distinct lump, altered breast size or appearance, inverted nipple, areola or skin changes, or skin dimpling. Promptly consult a doctor if you detect any such breast abnormalities.

  4. 04

    A urinary tract infection (UTI) is a bacterial infection that can occur in any part of the urinary system, typically affecting the bladder and urethra. Typical symptoms include painful urination, foul-smelling urine, and pelvic discomfort.
